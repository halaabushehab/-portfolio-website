MOCKUP-WIREEFRAME COFFEE

Description

This coffee website project is my first venture into web design, where I utilized Figma to create wireframes and mockups that highlight a clean, user-friendly interface. The goal was to design an engaging platform for coffee enthusiasts, featuring a menu display, online orders, and blog posts about coffee culture. The wireframes helped plan the structure, ensuring smooth navigation, while the mockups brought the concept to life with attractive visuals. This experience taught me the importance of balancing aesthetics with functionality. It’s a project I’m proud of, showcasing my ability to design from scratch with creativity and precision.
